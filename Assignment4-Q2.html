Courses at the Department of Computer Science

CS 265 Computer Science II

Covers object-oriented programming, generics, and applications of data structures such as lists, stacks, queues, and priority queues. Prerequisite: CS 155 with a minimum grade of C. (4)

CS 288 Computer System and Assembly Language

Computer system organization including logic circuits and assembly language. Prerequisites: CS 245 with a minimum grade of C. (4)

CS 300 Computer Science III

Covers object-oriented programming sets and maps, sorting, binary search trees, AVL trees, hashing, and graphs. Prerequisite: CS 265 with a minimum grade of C. (3)
