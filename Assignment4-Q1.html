Introductory courses at the Department of Computer Science

CS 101 Introduction to Computer Programming

Introduction to the design and development of simple programs using Python. (3)

CS 155 Computer Science I

Introduction to object-oriented programming in Java, with selection and repetition structures, pre-defined and programmer-defined functions, and arrays. Prerequisite: CS 101 with a minimum grade of C. (4)

CS 245 Discrete Structures I

Introduction to discrete structures as used in computer science. Topics include proof techniques, fundamental structures, and basic algorithm analysis. Prerequisite: CS 101 with a minimum grade of C. (3)
