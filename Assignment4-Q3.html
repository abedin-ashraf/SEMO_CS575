Courses offered at the Department of Computer Science

CS 345 Discrete Structures II

A continuation of the study of discrete structures in computer science. Topics may include graph theory, computational models, proof of correctness, and algorithm analysis. Prerequisites: CS 245 with a minimum grade of C. (3).

CS 350 Analysis of Algorithms

A continuation of the study of data abstractions and the algorithms for their manipulation. Emphasis on the analysis of non-numeric algorithms. Prerequisite: CS 300 and CS 345 with a minimum grade of C. (3).

CS 351 C and the Posix Environment

C language and Linux Environment. Prerequisite: CS 155 with a minimum grade of C. (4).
